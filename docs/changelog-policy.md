# Changelog Policy

## Purpose
Capture durable operational decisions and system changes tied to orchestration strategy.

## Categories
- `platform`: backend/service architecture
- `policy`: routing, budget, governance rules
- `model`: provider/model adoption decisions
- `infra`: deployment/runtime infrastructure
- `ops`: default operational updates

## Required Fields
- `summary`: one-line meaningful statement
- `category`: one of allowed categories

## Recommended Fields
- `details`: context, rationale, risks
- `actor`: automation or human owner

## Quality Bar
Entries should be auditable:
1. What changed.
2. Why it changed.
3. What impact is expected.
